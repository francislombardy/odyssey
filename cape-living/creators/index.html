FAQ

Creator program answers.

How do creators submit content?

Creators submit content through this Odyssey page by adding contact details, handles, content category, uploads, a caption, and the required rights and privacy consents.

Will creators be credited?

Yes. Cape Living and Odyssey will credit creators when selected submitted content is used organically.

Can creators get paid?

Selected creators may be matched with Cape Town businesses and brands for paid campaigns in future. Paid work depends on campaign fit, client needs, content quality, audience, and availability.

Will Cape Living use creator content for ads?

No. Submitted creator content will not be used for paid advertising unless the creator gives separate written permission.

What type of content is Cape Living looking for?

Cape Living is looking for Cape Town food, lifestyle, travel, events, nightlife, beauty, wellness, photography, videography, culture, places, local businesses, and hidden gems.

Cape Living by Odyssey

Cape Town discovery, powered by content, community, and data.

Cape Living is Odyssey's local discovery platform for Cape Town businesses, creators, venues, experiences, food, lifestyle, culture, events, tourism, and hidden gems.

While the standalone Cape Living website is being prepared for launch, Odyssey is opening early submissions for founding business features, creator content, and partnership opportunities.

Built to help Cape Town businesses get discovered.

Cape Living exists to showcase the places, people, products, experiences, restaurants, hotels, events, and local stories that make Cape Town worth exploring. Odyssey owns and operates the platform, which means every feature can be guided by the same data-led approach we use for marketing strategy: audience behaviour, content performance, SEO, AEO, GEO, and measurable outcomes.

FAQ

Cape Living early access answers.

Is Cape Living separate from Odyssey?

Cape Living is owned by Odyssey. A standalone Cape Living website is being prepared, but all early submissions, leads, and partnerships are currently handled through the Odyssey website.

Who can apply to be featured?

Cape Town businesses, venues, restaurants, hotels, creators, event organisers, lifestyle brands, tourism experiences, and culture-led projects can apply for early review.

How does Odyssey make Cape Living data-driven?

Odyssey uses audience behaviour, content performance, SEO, AEO, GEO, platform insights, and measurable reporting to guide features and campaigns.

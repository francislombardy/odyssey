FAQ

Business feature answers.

How do I get my business featured on Cape Living?

Submit the business form on this Odyssey page. Odyssey/Cape Living will review your business, media, location, audience fit, and feature readiness before publication or follow-up.

Is the founding business feature free?

Some early founding features may be complimentary during the launch phase. Paid content, sponsored features, advertising, creator campaigns, and partnership options may also be offered where relevant.

What type of businesses can apply?

Restaurants, cafes, bars, hotels, guesthouses, venues, local retailers, wellness studios, beauty businesses, event spaces, tourism experiences, lifestyle brands, and Cape Town service businesses can apply.

Can Cape Living create content for my business?

Yes. Odyssey can create photography, video, copy, SEO-friendly descriptions, campaign assets, and creator-led content for selected features and paid campaigns.

Will my business be promoted on Instagram and the newsletter?

Eligible features may include an Instagram feature, newsletter mention, profile link, and organic editorial visibility. The exact promotion depends on fit, timing, content quality, and campaign scope.
